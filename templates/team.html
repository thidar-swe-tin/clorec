Purpose:

What does Amazon client think about their purchased fashion product? Were they satisfied? What did they write in the review? Is there any product we can recommend next? In this project, three awesome powerpuffgirls investigated how Amazon client felt about their purchased product by conducting Exploratory Data Analysis & Sentiment Analysis, and suggesting the tailored product by building Recommendation Engine.
